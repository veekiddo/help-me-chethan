About Us

Looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life? Come aboard M/V Aegean Paradise, the bearer of entertainment!

New Century Group boasts more than two decades of service on the international waters of Batam, Indonesia, with Aegean Paradise as its third representative cruise in the line of leisure. Operating at all hours, M/V Aegean Paradise guarantees a rejuvenating stay with many recreational activities onboard, including friendly and helpful services to attend to your requests. A unique experience awaits.

As your preferred agent, we offer seamless, hassle-free ticketing services available here. Rest assured that all of your needs will be attended to before setting sail. Check out our monthly events and promotions for more details!
