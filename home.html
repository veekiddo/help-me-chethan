About Us

Looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life? Come aboard Aegean Paradise, the bearer of entertainment!

New Century Group boasts 20 years of service in various sectors, including cruise ship chartering services. Strategically located on the waters between Singapore and Batam, Aegean Paradise is our third representative cruise in the line of leisure. Operating at all hours of the day, Aegean Paradise guarantees a rejuvenating stay with several recreational activities to choose from.
