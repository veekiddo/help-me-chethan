Frequently Asked Questions

What documents do I need to present at the departure gate?

You will need to present your passport that is valid for at least six months, and PRINTED copies of the booking ticket.

Can I still book tickets one day before the ferry departure?

Yes, you may still book tickets the day before your intended departure, and 3 days before if the day of departure falls on the eve or day of public holiday.

Are tourists allowed onboard Aegean Paradise?

Only tourists whose stay in Singapore do not exceed 14 days are allowed to board the cruise. Otherwise, they must be holding a Multiple Journey visa as well as a return flight air ticket for their home country. Please refer to MOM website for more details.

What if I am a tourist and my stay in Singapore has been extended for more than 14 days?

Due to strict MOM regulations, we cannot make exceptions for such cases.

Can I make changes to my booking, or cancel it and get a refund?

There are no refunds for cancellation of tickets. There will be a S$5 charge for every change, and S$10 for each cancellation.

What is the baggage allowance?

Each individual is allowed up to 20kg of baggage allowance.

Will I be able to board the ship if I am pregnant?

Due to safety concerns, we do not permit expecting women to board the cruise.

Is it advisable to bring physically-disabled individuals onboard?

Due to the lack of handicap-friendly facilities on the ship, we do not allow them to board the cruise for their safety.

Is there a money changer onboard the cruise?

Yes. Currency exchange rates may vary onboard.

What modes of payment are accepted onboard?

We accept cash, Visa, and MasterCard. 4% surcharge applies for all purchases.
